Before Coming to the Store

Before visiting our Maid Cafe, here are a few things you should keep in mind to ensure a delightful experience:

  • Dress Code: Embrace the theme! While there's no strict dress code, many guests enjoy dressing in cute or themed outfits to enhance the experience

  • Etiquette: Respectful and courteous behavior towards our staff and fellow patrons is appreciated. Please refrain from taking photos without permission
